Tips to increase your commissions
1
Share the AI Advisor link directly — people who complete the quiz convert at much higher rates than cold browsers. Post it in fitness Facebook groups and Reddit.
2
Hit 3 sales in your first 180 days — Amazon requires this to keep your account. Ask friends or family to buy through your links first.
3
Focus on high-ticket products — Whey Protein ($48) and Lion's Mane ($40) earn more per click than budget items. Feature these prominently.
4
Amazon pays on everything in the cart — if someone clicks your supplement link and buys a TV too, you earn commission on the TV. Drive traffic, not just supplement shoppers.
5
Replace YOURTAG-20 in the store file with your real Amazon Associates tag or you won't get credited for any sales.
